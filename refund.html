Refund Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2026

1. Subscription Refunds

Monthly subscriptions (Basic and Pro plans) may be cancelled at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. We do not provide partial refunds for unused portions of a subscription period.

2. Lifetime Plan

Lifetime plan purchases are non-refundable once the service has been accessed. If you experience technical issues preventing use of the service, please contact us within 7 days of purchase.

3. Exceptions

We will provide a full refund within 7 days of purchase if: (a) the service is completely non-functional due to our error, or (b) you were charged incorrectly.

4. How to Request a Refund

Contact us at [email protected] with your order details. We will respond within 2 business days.

5. Processing

Approved refunds are processed through Paddle (our payment provider) and may take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement
